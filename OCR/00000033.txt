Treatment Report
1521 Luth WW
January 26, 2010
Kessler Collection
Description: Multiple signature textblock, sewn on three recessed cords.
Condition: The sewing is rotten and broken. There are tears at the fore edge of
the title page and there is an area of paper loss at the top corner of the fifth page.
Treatment: Mended tears and filled loss areas with Japanese tissue and wheat
starch paste. Replaced sewing with three linen cords and linen thread. Added new
end papers made of 60 lb. Mowhawk Vellum paper. . Constructed a new cover
using lignin-free board and Canapetta book cloth.
